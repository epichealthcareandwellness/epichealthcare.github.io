Meet the Founder

Dr. Michelle Grimes

DNP · Family NP-BC
30+ Years in Nursing
Legal Nurse Consultant
AHA Ambassador

Dr. Michelle Grimes is a doctoral-prepared board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with over 30 years in nursing. She earned her DNP from Chamberlain University (2017), MSN & FNP from University of Missouri St. Louis (2014), BSN from UMSL (2002), MBA (2004), and ADN from Forest Park Community College (1987).

Michelle is the former CEO/President/Founder of the St. Louis Chapter of Black Nurses Rock, an American Heart Association Ambassador, a Stop the Bleed instructor, and a member of the Association of Missouri Nurse Practitioners Advocacy Committee. She founded the Education Athletes & Heart Health Foundation (501c3).

She currently serves as Chief Nursing Officer for CareSTL Health. She is a wife, mother of three, and grandmother of eleven.

"Let's not take the care out of health!! I am a voice for those that don't have one." — Dr. Michelle Grimes, DNP, FNP-BC

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll as a new patient?+
Complete our New Patient Enrollment Form in the Patient Portal, or call us at (636) 789-1499.
Do you offer mobile or in-home services?+
Yes! EPIC provides mobile services throughout O'Fallon and the greater St. Louis Metropolitan Area.
What states do you offer telehealth in?+
Iowa, Colorado, Kansas, Missouri, Nebraska, Illinois, Texas, and Connecticut via PushHealth.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept debit and credit cards, CashApp, and Venmo in addition to all accepted insurance plans.
What is the family enrollment discount?+
Families who enroll together receive 10% off our services. Call (636) 789-1499 to get started.
What is the EPIC Glow Facial?+
Our hydrodermabrasion facial treatments — The Refresh ($89/30min), The Revive ($149/60min), and The Transformation ($219/75min). Zero downtime, immediate results.
Do you offer med spa and aesthetic services?+
Yes! We offer EPIC Glow facials, skin rejuvenation, body contouring, injectables, and more. Complete an Aesthetic Services Consent to get started.
Is the facility wheelchair accessible?+
Absolutely. We are wheelchair accessible and child-friendly, committed to an inclusive environment for all patients.
