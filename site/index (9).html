Meet the Founder

Dr. Michelle Grimes

DNP · Family NP-BC
30+ Years in Nursing
Legal Nurse Consultant
AHA Ambassador

Dr. Michelle Grimes is a doctoral-prepared board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with over 30 years in nursing. She earned a terminal degree in Advanced Practice Leadership from Chamberlain University (2017), her MSN & FNP from University of Missouri St. Louis (2014), her BSN from UMSL (2002), her MBA (2004), and her ADN from Forest Park Community College (1987).

Michelle is the former CEO/President/Founder of the St. Louis Chapter of Black Nurses Rock, an American Heart Association Ambassador, a Stop the Bleed instructor, and a member of the Association of Missouri Nurse Practitioners Advocacy Committee — advocating for removal of barriers to healthcare. She founded the Education Athletes & Heart Health Foundation (501c3), focused on preventing undiagnosed heart conditions through education, physicals including EKGs, and community awareness.

She currently serves as Chief Nursing Officer for CareSTL Health — an organization of Federally Qualified Health Centers with four freestanding clinics and four school-based healthcare centers across multiple school districts. She is a wife, mother of three, and grandmother of eleven.

"Let's not take the care out of health!! I am a voice for those that don't have one." — Dr. Michelle Grimes, DNP, FNP-BC

Our Purpose

Transforming Healthcare
One Community
at a Time

Epic Healthcare and Wellness Solutions is not merely a service provider — it's a movement to reshape expectations in healthcare for every population and ethnic group. Female-owned, family-owned, and deeply rooted in the St. Louis community, we accept insurance, offer house calls, and prioritize patient comfort at every step.

Mission Statement
Assuring access to healthcare and social services for the underserved in the St. Louis Metropolitan Area and community. Bringing affordable healthcare to you, wherever you are. To increase the accessibility of primary care in all areas, including the underserved areas, while transforming healthcare and decreasing health disparities and inequalities while shaping the healthy behaviors and environments of those we serve.
Vision Statement
We promise to empower patients to take control of their healthcare, to offer and provide healthcare and wellness which promotes the need of the individual. To advocate for those in need while arming them with tools that promote healthy lifestyle changes with an end outcome being one of longevity of life, healthcare empowerment and better disease management knowledge.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll as a new patient?
Complete our New Patient Enrollment Form in the Patient Portal above, or call us at (636) 789-1499 and we'll walk you through the process.
Do you offer mobile or in-home services?
Yes! EPIC provides mobile services throughout O'Fallon and the greater St. Louis Metropolitan Area. We bring care directly to your door.
What states do you offer telehealth in?
We currently see patients via telehealth in Iowa, Colorado, Kansas, Missouri, and Nebraska through our secure PushHealth portal.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept debit and credit cards, CashApp, and Venmo in addition to all accepted insurance plans.
What is the family enrollment discount?
Families who enroll together receive 10% off our services. Complete the enrollment form or call (636) 789-1499 to get started.
What documents do I need before my first visit?
Please complete your New Patient Enrollment, Treatment Consent, and HIPAA Privacy Authorization before your visit. Bring your insurance card and photo ID.
Do you offer med spa and aesthetic services?
Yes! As a Medical Spa & Aesthetics Collective, we offer skin rejuvenation, body contouring, injectables, and more. Complete an Aesthetic Services Consent to get started.
Is the facility wheelchair accessible?
Absolutely. We are wheelchair accessible, child-friendly, and committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive environment for all patients.
